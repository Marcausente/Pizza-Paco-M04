About us

Enter the exciting gastronomic world of Pizzas Paco, a restaurant that goes beyond simple fast food to offer you a unique culinary experience. Located in the heart of the city, Pizzas Paco welcomes you with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, creating the perfect setting to enjoy a delicious variety of pizzas, pastas and other accompanying products.

Our menu is a tribute to Italian tradition, highlighting the freshness and authenticity of the ingredients we use in each dish. From the classic Margherita to the most innovative creations, our pizzas are true works of gastronomic art that delight all the senses. The dough, finely baked, combines perfectly with a selection of sauces and toppings that highlight the most authentic flavors of Italian cuisine.

The pasta section is equally impressive, offering everything from the most comforting options to the most sophisticated. From Spaghetti Bolognese, which transport you straight to the streets of Rome, to Lasañas, which envelop you in layers of delicious flavors, each dish reflects our chefs' passion for authenticity and quality.

But the Pizzas Paco experience doesn't stop there. Our menu also includes a variety of side products that perfectly complement your main choice. The crispy, golden fries, as well as the onion rings, are irresistible options that add a delicious touch to your dining experience.

At Pizzas Paco, we pride ourselves on not only offering exceptional food, but also creating special moments for our customers. Our friendly and committed service team is always ready to make every visit an unforgettable experience.

Whether you're looking for a quick lunch with friends, a family dinner or a special occasion, Pizzas Paco is the perfect place to satisfy your cravings for quality Italian food in a cozy and charming setting. Come and immerse yourself in the delicious fusion of tradition and modernity in our beloved restaurant, where every bite is a celebration of good food and good company. We are waiting for you at Pizzas Paco to share memorable moments and authentic flavors!
